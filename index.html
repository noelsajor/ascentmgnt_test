Studio Project

Brand & Storefront for a DTC Skincare Label

The Brief

A clean beauty brand needed a complete visual identity and Shopify presence that communicated premium quality without pretension. The existing site felt generic and didn't reflect the product's thoughtful formulation.

Design Decisions

  • Developed a restrained typographic system with generous whitespace to emphasize product photography
  • Created a brand mark and color palette that felt modern and clinical without being cold
  • Built custom Shopify sections for ingredient transparency and editorial product stories
  • Simplified product hierarchy and checkout flow to reduce cognitive load

Deliverables

Brand Identity System Packaging Design Custom Shopify Theme Product Photography Direction Content Templates

Outcome

The brand now communicates premium quality at every touchpoint. Customers report feeling more confident in their purchase decisions. The store loads quickly, feels premium, and supports a smoother purchase journey from discovery to checkout.

Common Questions

Do you handle branding and packaging as well as Shopify?

Yes. We offer full brand identity systems, packaging design, and Shopify development. Most of our clients work with us across multiple touchpoints to ensure visual coherence from product to web to print.

Can you work with our existing theme or do we need a rebuild?

We can assess your current theme and determine the best path forward. Sometimes customization is sufficient; other times a custom build is necessary to achieve the desired quality. We're transparent about what's required.

How do you keep the store fast and accessible with custom design?

Performance and accessibility are built in from the start. We write clean, semantic code, optimize assets, and follow best practices for Core Web Vitals and WCAG standards. Quality design and fast load times aren't mutually exclusive.

What do you need from us to get started?

An introductory call, access to current brand assets (if they exist), product information, and clarity on goals and timeline. We handle the rest — from strategy through execution.

Do you collaborate with in-house teams?

Absolutely. We work with in-house designers, developers, and marketers regularly. Clear communication and mutual respect for craft make these collaborations successful.

What's your typical project timeline?

Brand projects take 6–8 weeks. Full Shopify builds take 8–12 weeks. Timelines depend on scope and revision rounds, but we're transparent about schedule from the start.

Do you work with brands outside the US?

Yes. We work with clients globally. Most communication is asynchronous, and we're experienced coordinating across time zones.
