Growth Case Study

32% CAC Reduction Through Creative + CRO Alignment

The Problem

A DTC skincare brand was spending heavily on Meta ads but couldn't scale profitably. Creative was disconnected from landing page experience. CAC was climbing, ROAS was unstable, and checkout abandonment was 62%.

What We Fixed

  • Rebuilt ad creative strategy to match landing page messaging and product hierarchy
  • Optimized product pages for mobile, reduced checkout steps from 5 to 3
  • Implemented strategic bundling on PDPs, increased AOV from $47 to $63
  • Set up proper attribution and conversion tracking across Meta and Google

The Results

CAC down 32% ROAS stable at 4.1x AOV up 34% $140k added monthly revenue

Key Insight

CAC improved because creative finally matched the landing page experience. Visitors arrived expecting what they saw in ads, found it immediately, and converted. Scaling spend became profitable because post-click conversion held strong.

Common Questions

How is this different from hiring separate agencies for ads and CRO?

We connect acquisition with conversion from day one. Most agencies run ads without optimizing the landing experience, or build stores without considering how traffic arrives. We align creative, landing pages, and checkout flows so acquisition actually converts.

Do you handle social media content or just paid ads?

Both. We build content systems (strategy, calendars, creative direction, posting, community management) that support brand presence and conversion. We also run paid campaigns across Meta, TikTok, and Google. The two work together to drive consistent growth.

What if our store is already built? Can you just optimize it?

Absolutely. We assess your current store and determine what's needed — sometimes it's minor CRO fixes, other times a full rebuild is required to hit your growth goals. We're transparent about what's necessary and why.

How quickly can we expect results?

Most clients see measurable improvements within 30–45 days. Ad performance and CRO changes can show impact immediately if traffic volume is sufficient. Full store builds take 6–10 weeks with ongoing optimization post-launch.

Why focus on CAC and ROAS instead of just driving more traffic?

Traffic means nothing if it doesn't convert profitably. We optimize for business metrics — CAC, ROAS, CVR, AOV, LTV — because that's what actually grows revenue. More traffic with poor conversion is just wasted spend.

Do you work with brands outside the US?

Yes. We work with DTC brands globally. Most communication is asynchronous, and we're experienced coordinating across time zones and currency requirements.

What's included in your Growth Partnership retainer?

Ongoing paid ads management, social content strategy and posting, CRO and store optimization, creative production, performance reporting, and strategic reviews. Think of it as a full growth team without the overhead of hiring in-house.
